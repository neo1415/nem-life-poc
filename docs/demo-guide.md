# NEM Life+ Demo Guide

## Recommended Order

1. Open `/demo/executive`.
2. Start with Chinedu Eze, the business owner scenario.
3. Show `/demo/scenarios/business_owner`.
4. Jump to result, report, dashboard, admin leads, then config preview.
5. Use `/demo/scenarios/compare` to show segmentation across personas.
6. Use `/demo/reset` before a fresh presentation.

## What Each Persona Proves

- Tunde Adebayo: cross-sell from motor insurance into life and health.
- Amaka Okorie: top-up and family review for partial employer cover.
- Chinedu Eze: life, health, business, and readiness opportunity in one journey.
- Bisi Lawal: retention and review value for better protected customers.
- Sade Bello: guidance-first support for unsure customers.

## Presenter Notes

Say that every score is estimated from demo answers. Say that final eligibility, pricing, and cover depend on approved NEM rules. Say that no live NEM systems, CRM, policy, payment, email, advisor assignment, underwriting, or claims workflow is connected.

Avoid saying the data is verified, live, approved by compliance, synced to CRM, sent by email, assigned to an advisor, paid, issued, or guaranteed.

## Reset

Use `/demo/reset` to clear browser-stored NEM Life+ demo session, lead, scenario, and config draft keys. It does not touch any live system.
