# AI Guardrails

AI is not required for POC v1. Use deterministic templates.

The rule is: the system calculates; AI explains.

Future AI may explain scores, summarize gaps, draft report language, help admins inspect messy data, or suggest mappings when explicitly approved. AI must not approve claims, make underwriting decisions, merge records automatically, calculate final premiums outside approved rules, receive BVN/NIN unnecessarily, expose sensitive data, replace regulated insurance advice, or pretend final policy approval.
