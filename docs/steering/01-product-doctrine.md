# Product Doctrine

## Product Philosophy

NEM Life+ is not a throwaway demo. It must be secure by default, modular, readable, testable, extensible, enterprise-ready in structure, and easy for a senior developer to continue.

## Product Thesis

People do not wake up wanting to buy life insurance. They want to make sure their family will be okay if something happens.

NEM already has customers, products, trust, agents, and digital channels. NEM Life+ helps turn those strengths into life insurance growth, cross-sell opportunities, premium continuity, and better customer intelligence.

## POC Scope

The POC includes a landing page, guided Family Protection Check, estimated score, human score explanation, recommended protection plan, lead capture, report preview, customer dashboard preview, admin lead dashboard, configurable questions, scoring, recommendation rules, demo data, and clear future integration surfaces.

The POC must not include real NEM database integration, CRM sync, payment, BVN/NIN verification, document upload, policy issuance, underwriting, claims processing, live WhatsApp/SMS/email sending, or a regulated AI adviser.

## CTA Doctrine

Primary CTAs may include View Recommended Plans, Start My Protection Plan, Start Registration, Get This Cover, and Continue to NEM Life. Support CTAs may include Speak With a NEM Advisor, Send My Report, and Save My Result. Do not make booking a call the only path.

## Estimated Language

Always make clear that scores are estimated, pricing is illustrative, eligibility depends on approved NEM rules, no live NEM records have been checked, no policy has been issued, and no final premium is guaranteed.
